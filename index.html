ABOUT
Around the world, countries have adapted health policies of different magnitudes ever since their exposure to SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID-19. Cough Cough is an interactive game in which players can learn and discover the varied mitigation policies undertaken by different nations during the COVID-19 pandemic while making decisions as a health director of their fictional nation.
How to Play (maybe add a lil icon here)
Imagine you are the health director of a fictional nation and you have just received classified news of the pandemic. You will be given a number of decisions to make that will affect your nationâ€™s virus statistics. If the death count reaches above 25% of the population (250,000), the hospital capacity overfills, or the virus positivity rate surpasses 50% (meaning that 1 in 2 people has the virus), the pandemic is out of control and you lose. Survive the pandemic by maintaining the health of your nation for 12 months.
